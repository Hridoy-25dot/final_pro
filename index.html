Team History

Founded in 2015, Team Economics BUP made history by becoming the first non-BBA team to reach the Inter BUP Football Final in their debut season, proudly finishing as runners-up. What followed was nothing short of legendary.

Fueled by unbreakable team spirit and relentless training, the team clinched the championship title the very next season—and never looked back. They went on to dominate every tournament thereafter, shattering the long-standing monopoly of BBA in university football.

They are the only department in BUP to have never conceded a single goal during match time — a record that speaks volumes about their discipline, strategy, and brotherhood.

Today, Team Economics BUP stands as a symbol of determination, unity, and unstoppable drive, inspiring a new generation of players to dream big and fight harder.
